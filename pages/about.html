What is Vincent OS?

Vincent OS is a GNU/Linux distribution made by v38armageddon.


What is the features of Vincent OS?

The features of Vincent OS are:

  • A customized Kernel for 100% compatibility of Windows Application.
  • Very customization and lightweight as possible.
  • ArchLinux based distribution.
